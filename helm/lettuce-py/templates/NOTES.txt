Lettuce Python Temporal worker has been deployed.

To check the status of the worker:
  kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "lettuce-py.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}"

To view logs:
  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "lettuce-py.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl logs --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} $POD_NAME
